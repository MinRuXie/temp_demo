FAQ

AndMakers is a subsidiary company of Cenports Commerce Inc. We connect global manufacturers and brands with U.S. retailers. We offer three core services: sales (AndMakers), warehousing & fulfillment (AndFulfillment), and a SaaS platform for inventory automation (Cenports).

We have an 80,000 sq. foot warehouse located in the San Francisco Bay Area. Our warehouse has a flat-fee based fulfillment model and provides volume-based storage discounts.

Additionally, our platform is already integrated with 30+ major U.S. retailer partners. We can support your entry into the U.S. market and guide you through every step of the way.

Last, we offer a real-time sales and analytics dashboard to help you automate shipments, inventory, and order management via our proprietary cloud-based technology, which is connected with all major retailers.

In order to enter the U.S. market without a partner, your company will need to invest in expensive IT systems, hire expensive sales reps to connect with retailers, search for reliable warehouse and fulfillment services, hire 24/7 customer service, and likely set up a U.S. entity and U.S. bank account.
If your company does not have experience selling in the U.S. and is operating on a limited budget, AndMakers is the best choice for easy, low-cost access to build up your e-commerce business in the U.S., gain industry connections, and let you use your resources to grow revenue.

We offer a one-stop solution for vendors to enter the U.S. market and we allow you to focus on producing while we take care of the rest. We offer storage, fulfillment, marketing, branding, a local customer service team, and immediate connections to major U.S. retailers such as Wayfair, Home Depot, Walmart, Lowe’s, and many more.

In your first meeting with the AndMakers team, we will evaluate your products and provide you with a profit & loss analysis. Once we confirm your products meet U.S. standards, we are ready to start preparing for selling!

When the products arrive at our warehouse, we will work to upload them through all of our retail channels and build a marketing strategy.

After orders have sold, you’ll be able to check order details and shipment statuses on our dashboard. Payment will be available once the monthly invoice is provided.

Our business model is commission-based; any other fees are due to warehousing and fulfillment, or from additional services such as insurance and branding.

Since retailers have their own return and replacement policies, vendors are required to provide an allowance to the retailers. The allowance is a percentage of the wholesale price per unit.

Once we get product information into our system, we can get your products listed with different retailers within days. Our status as a Wayfair Preferred Partner enables quick onboarding with the fastest-growing home goods platform in eCommerce. (Note that some retailers need up to 2 weeks to approve products.)
