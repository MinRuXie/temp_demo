Our goal is to help you to establish your business in online retail. Our process for building your brand’s success is as follows:

Evaluation

This step is to review your product and samples. With our combined 30+ years of eCommerce experience, we evaluate the market fit of your product and provide you with a competitive analysis and forecast. At this stage, we will also review with you our terms.

Strategy

The next step is to create your retailer and selling strategy focusing on projected growth. At this stage, we also hone in on your product’s description and images.

Selling

With your foundation set and your product information ready, we’ll upload your products to our sellers. Your product is now ready to be sold!

Once we get product information into our system, we can get your products listed with different retailers within days. Our status as a Wayfair Preferred Partner enables quick onboarding with the fastest-growing home goods platform in eCommerce. (Note that some retailers need up to 2 weeks to approve products.)
